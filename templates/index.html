What is Forge QR?

Forge QR is a free QR code generator that lets you create fully customized QR codes in seconds — no account, no watermark, no limits. Choose between static QR codes (permanent, encode data directly) or dynamic QR codes (editable destination URL, with scan tracking). You can also generate App Store QR codes that automatically redirect iOS and Android users to the right store.

Customize every detail: foreground and background colors, dot style (square, rounded, circle, bars), eye style, container shape (square, rounded, circle), frame text, font, and even your own logo. Add a shadow or 3D effect to make your QR code stand out on print or digital media.

Supported content types include URLs, plain text, WiFi credentials, email, phone numbers, SMS, vCard contacts, and social media profiles. Download your QR code as a high-resolution PNG, ready for print or digital use.
