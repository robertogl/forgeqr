What is Forge QR?

Forge QR is a free online QR code generator built for creators, marketers, and businesses. No account required, no watermarks, no usage limits. Generate professional QR codes in seconds and — with dynamic codes — track exactly who scans them, from where, and on what device.

🎨

Full Customization

Colors, dot styles, corner eyes, shapes (square, rounded, circle), shadow & 3D effects, frame labels in 16 fonts, edge borders, and your own logo — every detail is in your hands.

🔄

Editable Destinations

Dynamic QR codes let you update the destination URL any time without reprinting. Change a campaign landing page, swap a menu, or fix a broken link — the printed QR stays the same.

📊

Built-In Scan Analytics

Every dynamic QR code includes a private stats dashboard: total scans over time, top countries & cities, device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), OS, and browser. No third-party tools, no extra cost.

✨

Standard or AI Art Style

Pick a clean Standard QR with full visual control, or generate an AI Art QR — a stunning AI-generated image that doubles as a scannable code. Both fully free, no signup.
