App Store QR codes are smart redirect codes that send each user to the right destination automatically — iPhone and iPad users go to the Apple App Store, Android users to Google Play, and everyone else (desktop, unknown devices) to a fallback URL of your choice. One QR code covers all platforms, making it ideal for app download campaigns, posters, and packaging.

Bulk QR codes let you generate many QR codes at once from a CSV or Excel file. Perfect for restaurant chains with multiple locations, marketing campaigns with personalized URLs, asset tagging, event tickets, or any case where you need lots of unique QR codes. Upload a file with one URL per row, choose the visual style, and download a ZIP file containing all generated QR codes as PNGs.
📄 File format Your file should have one URL per row. Optionally include a second column with a label (used as the filename in the ZIP).
Example CSV: 
url,label
https://example.com/london,London_Menu
https://example.com/paris,Paris_Menu
https://example.com/rome,Rome_Menu
Maximum 200 QR codes per upload.

What is Forge QR?

Forge QR is a free online QR code generator built for creators, marketers, and businesses. No account required, no watermarks, no usage limits. Generate professional QR codes in seconds and — with dynamic codes — track exactly who scans them, from where, and on what device.

🎨

Full Customization

Colors, dot styles, corner eyes, shapes (square, rounded, circle), shadow & 3D effects, frame labels in 16 fonts, edge borders, and your own logo — every detail is in your hands.

🔄

Editable Destinations

Dynamic QR codes let you update the destination URL any time without reprinting. Change a campaign landing page, swap a menu, or fix a broken link — the printed QR stays the same.

📊

Built-In Scan Analytics

Every dynamic QR code includes a private stats dashboard: total scans over time, top countries & cities, device type (mobile, tablet, desktop), OS, and browser. No third-party tools, no extra cost.
