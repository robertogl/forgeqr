App Store QR codes are smart redirect codes that send each user to the right destination automatically — iPhone and iPad users go to the Apple App Store, Android users to Google Play, and everyone else (desktop, unknown devices) to a fallback URL of your choice. One QR code covers all platforms, making it ideal for app download campaigns, posters, and packaging.

Bulk QR codes let you generate many QR codes at once from a CSV or Excel file. Perfect for restaurant chains with multiple locations, marketing campaigns with personalized URLs, asset tagging, event tickets, or any case where you need lots of unique QR codes. Upload a file with one URL per row, choose the visual style, and download a ZIP file containing all generated QR codes as PNGs.
