About Forge QR

A free, no-nonsense QR code generator built for people who want professional results without the friction.

What is Forge QR?

Forge QR is a free online QR code generator built for marketers, small business owners, designers, and anyone who needs a QR code that actually looks good. No account required, no watermarks, no hidden limits — just generate, customize, and download.

We built Forge QR because most QR generators are either too basic (plain black squares with no options) or too expensive (paywalled behind SaaS subscriptions). Our goal is to give everyone access to professional-grade QR codes for free, whether you're printing menus, running campaigns, or sharing your Wi-Fi password.

What makes it different?

🎨

Full visual control

Colors, dot styles, eye shapes, container shapes, 3D effects, frame labels, edge lines, and your own logo — all in one place.

🔄

Dynamic & editable

Change the destination URL of any dynamic QR code at any time, without reprinting. Ideal for campaigns, menus, and packaging.

📊

Built-in analytics

Every dynamic QR comes with a private stats page: scans by day, country, city, device, OS, and browser — no third-party tools needed.

📱

Smart app redirects

App Store QR codes automatically send iOS users to the App Store and Android users to Google Play — one code for all platforms.

🔒

Privacy-first

Static QR codes store no data at all. Dynamic codes only collect anonymous scan metadata — no personal information, ever.

🆓

Genuinely free

No sign-up, no watermark, no credit card, no "free trial". Every feature on this site is free for everyone, always.

Our mission

We believe great tools should be accessible to everyone — not just companies with marketing budgets. Whether you're a freelancer making a business card, a restaurant owner updating a menu, or a developer testing an app download flow, Forge QR is built for you.

We're constantly improving the tool based on user feedback. If there's a feature you'd love to see, or something that's not working right, we genuinely want to hear about it.
